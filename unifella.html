About Unifella

Unifella is an online university application website mainly focused on Chinese websites. Recently we have witnessed huge numbers of international students developing interests to pursue their higher education in Chinese universities myself being one of them. But acute lack of information such as tuition fees structure and various aspects of life in China hinders the efforts of hundreds of students worldwide from reaching for their dreams. Unifella seeks to become the online platform that students can visit to get all information about universities such as tuition fees structure, accommodation status, university rankings, and comments from other students. Unifella also enables students to apply directly to universities without going through middlemen to cut down on unnecessary costs and time.

Technologies

Show case

All information on the platform in available without restrictions but users are required to create an account in order to apply for universities or scolarships.

Users can track the status of their application from their user profile page, users can also also cancel unwanted applications.

Unifella website also provides detailed information about all programs and scholarships available in universities, information such as application requirements, visa requirements, steps on how to apply, eligibility as well as fequent asked questions.

Unifella website features an admin dashboard where administrators can view and edit information about universities and cities, admin dashboard also enables administrators to accept or reject applications from students
